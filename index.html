Privacy Policy

This policy explains how RepsAura ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards the information you provide when using our fitness tracking application. By using RepsAura, you agree to the practices described here.

01

Introduction

RepsAura is a personal fitness and nutrition tracking application available on Android. Our mission is to help you build better habits through data you own and control. We are committed to being transparent about what we collect and why.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the RepsAura mobile application and any related services. If you have questions about any part of this policy, contact us at any time using the information in Section 10.

Plain-language summary: We collect the data you enter to make the app work. We do not sell your personal data. We do not share it with advertisers. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
02

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and password (used to create and authenticate your account)
  • Display name and optional profile photo
  • Referral code generated uniquely for your account

Fitness & Health Data

  • Exercise logs: workout sessions, exercises performed, sets, reps, and weights
  • Personal records (PRs) for individual exercises
  • Route data and GPS coordinates collected during outdoor activity tracking (Walk, Run, Cycling) — only while the GPS feature is actively enabled
  • Progress photos you choose to upload, tagged by body part and date
  • Body weight measurements and goals (weight gain, loss, or maintenance)

Nutrition Data

  • Foods you add to your personal food list, including nutritional values and price
  • Daily calorie and macronutrient logs
  • Meal times and food quantities
  • AI-generated recipes and any notes saved within the AI Recipe Chef feature

Usage & Technical Data

  • Device push notification token (for workout reminders and app alerts)
  • Subscription status and purchase receipt tokens processed via Google Play
  • App usage patterns (which features you access) to improve the product
  • Personal notes you write within the app

Community Data

  • Posts, comments, and reactions you share in community feeds or groups
  • Your public profile information if you choose to make it visible

04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers hosted via Render (render.com), a managed cloud infrastructure provider based in the United States. Data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text.

Progress photos are stored securely and are only accessible to your account. GPS route data is retained on our servers and is never shared with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.

While we implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorised access.

Data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

05

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in these circumstances:

  • With service providers who process data on our behalf under data processing agreements (see Section 6 for the full list)
  • With other users only for content you explicitly make public in the Community section
  • For legal compliance if required by applicable law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users
  • In a business transfer if RepsAura is acquired or merges with another entity — you will be notified in advance

Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data at all times:

  • Access & export — you can view all data you have entered directly within the app
  • Correction — you can edit any information in your profile or logs at any time
  • Deletion — you can delete individual logs, photos, or your entire account from Settings → Delete Account
  • Notifications — you can disable push notifications at any time via your device settings or within the app
  • GPS tracking — location data is only collected when you explicitly enable GPS tracking for an activity; you can turn it off at any time
  • Community visibility — you control whether your profile is public or private

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA respectively. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at repsaurasupport@gmail.com.

08

Children's Privacy

RepsAura is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at repsaurasupport@gmail.com and we will delete the information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, sending a notification through the app.

Your continued use of RepsAura after any changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
